Aqua-Plus Prins System

Revolutionary Pre-Insulated Technology

The Aqua-Plus Prins system represents a quantum leap in pre-insulated BETA polypropylene (PP-R/PP-RCT) pipes and fittings. This advanced, fully certified solution is engineered for maximum thermal efficiency and sustainability.

Compatible with existing moulded components, the system guarantees simple, secure connections whilst delivering optimum reduction of wasted energy across all applications.
